I am a communicator and theologian, educated in Iceland and Sweden.

I lead the communciations for The Lutheran World Federation, a communion of 150 churches in the Lutheran tradition. Together we are working to make the world more just and peaceful.

In my work I combine practice and research. I have a passion for the intersection between communication, culture, technology and society, to which I bring a faith and justice perspective. I have researched, lectured and taught on church, culture, and communication.

I have also tried my hand at different media, print, web, tv and radio, but have a particular interest in online communities and communication.

Also, I'm from Iceland. Does that make me unique?
